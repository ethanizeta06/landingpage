Random facts about animals

Rabbits can't vomit! Their digestive system only moves in one direction, so they rely on a super efficient gut to keep things flowing smoothly.

Polar bears have black skin under their thick white fur, which helps them absorb heat from the sun and stay warm in icy environments.

Cats can make over 100 different vocal sounds, while dogs only have about 10—talk about being chatty when they want something!

Squirrels plant thousands of acorns every year and often forget where they hid them—accidentally helping forests grow in the process.
